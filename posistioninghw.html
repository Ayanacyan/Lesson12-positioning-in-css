Virtually Me

Virtually Me is one of the books from the most infamous lonestar list for 2024
Virtually Me's main protagonists are Bradley, Edelsabeth, Jasper, Hunter, and Keiko.
It's genre is realistic fiction
It is about 5 kids who wish to hide their real selves from the world for many different reasons and the school offered people who still don't feel safe after the pandemic a VR schooling offer
As they meet, they form a team to win a tournament full of vr gamesand they become a close team, but when they meet eachother they find out who all of them really are
