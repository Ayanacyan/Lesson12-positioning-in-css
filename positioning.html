The Nervous System

The Nervous System is one of the most important and complex systems in our body
It has many organs though the most famous is ofcourse our brain
These organs and cells can be divided into 2 major groups: The CNS and the ANS
The CNS is made up of the main control center of our body which is the brain and the main highway of messages in our body which is the spinal cord
The PNS is made up of many important cells like neurons which make up our nerves
